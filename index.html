ABOUT US

Spices are natural plant-derived substances, including seeds, fruits, roots, bark, and other parts, primarily used for flavoring, coloring, or preserving food.
They are also known for their medicinal properties and are used in various applications, including pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and religious rituals. Spices are distinguished from herbs, which are the leaves, flowers, or stems of plants used for flavoring or as a garnish. Spices and seasoning do not mean the same thing, but spices fall under the seasoning category with herbs. Spices are sometimes used in medicine, religious rituals, cosmetics, or perfume production.
Chili Powder:
A spice blend made from dried and ground chili peppers, used to add heat and flavor to dishes
Black Pepper:
A spice with a pungent, peppery flavor, used in both savory and sweet dishes
Turmeric:
A spice with a bright yellow color and a slightly peppery flavor, used in both savory and sweet dishes, and known for its medicinal properties
